Membership

Any blind individual above 18 years of age may become a member of Blind Persons’ Association. According to Rule 3 of the Constitution of the organisation:

Members of the Association shall consist of blind persons, aged 18 or above, desirous of becoming members of the Association and willing to pay membership subscription, as decided from time to time in the General Meeting of the Association. Any blind person, who is eligible for membership, may become Life Member by paying a one-time subscription, decided from time to time in the General Meeting.

At present annual subscription for members is INR 6. Subscription for life membership is INR 250.

If you are a blind person and willing to join us, download Membership Form or Life Membership Form here. Fill in the form and send it to the office by post and wait for confirmation. Please do not forget to mention your email address and / or contact number. You will be notified about payment of subscription if it is accepted.

You need not send your subscription until you are asked to do so. We do not have any extra admission fee. Subscription for the first year or subscription for life is enough for enrollment.
