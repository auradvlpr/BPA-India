We provide Braille books to blind students in India for INR 10 per Braille volume. Institutions can buy these books at paper cost. Our objective is to ensure that visually impaired students have access to Braille books at an affordable price. Braille books are hardly available in India on account of their huge production cost. The following list consists of the paper cost of the books produced at the Braille press at Lal Bihari Shah Braille Academia. HOW TO BUY BRAILLE BOOKS

Order for Braille books may be sent to the Braille press at:

Lal Bihari Shah Braille Academia
Bidyapalli, Malancha-Mahinagar
Kolkata – 700145
Phone: 9433124108
Email: bpaindia@gmail.com

Send your order with payment in advance in demand draft or money order. Payment should include INR 30 as shipping and handling charge for every three Braille volumes. Processing may take a week or two, subject to the number of Braille volumes, postal service, etc. We use postage-free service for Braille literature offered by the post offices. Still, we have to charge extra for packing books and sending them to a distant post office.

We supply Braille books anywhere in India. We need your full mailing address with postal code.

Demand drafts should be drawn in favor of Blind Persons’ Association payable in any scheduled bank in Kolkata. Orders or demand drafts may preferably be sent using government postal service.

You may also make payment using net banking facility directly to this bank account:

Send us an email intimating your transaction ID, date, and amount, list of books, and full delivery details.
